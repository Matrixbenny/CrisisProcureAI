# World-Class Roadmap

This roadmap is designed to move CrisisProcure AI from strong hackathon MVP to globally deployable, trusted agentic operations platform.

## Phase 1: Trust and Governance (0-3 months)
- Policy pack schema validation with CI checks
- Correlation IDs across agent responses and BPMN tasks
- Explainability snapshots persisted in process audit records
- Role-based approval controls by policy tier

## Phase 2: Resilience and Scale (3-6 months)
- Surge simulation endpoint to stress-test prioritization
- Circuit-breaker and fallback queue patterns for external dependencies
- Tenant-aware policy packs for regional governance variants
- Priority recalculation loop for dynamic crisis updates

## Phase 3: Global Adaptation (6-12 months)
- Multilingual intake and operator-facing guidance
- Sector-specific policy templates: healthcare, utilities, logistics, public response
- Accessibility and low-bandwidth interaction mode
- Supplier reliability scoring and disruption forecasting

## Phase 4: Advanced Intelligence (12+ months)
- Scenario digital twin for procurement planning
- Agent negotiation copilot with hard policy constraints
- Cross-organization coordination APIs for emergency mutual aid
- Outcome optimization for speed, cost, and policy confidence

## Success metrics to track
- Median request-to-PO cycle time
- Time-to-approval for high-risk requests
- Percentage of requests with full explainability trace
- Percentage of runs resolved without manual rework
- Prevented stockout events attributable to prioritization
